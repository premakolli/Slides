Introduction

The application is meant to provide a simple visualization for the Property Hunter Application. . This application is for property investors to calculate the rental yield of a property and estimate the cashflow of owning the property. Your real estate agent and mortgage broker will be able to help you with the numbers. . There are mainly two subset of fields, Property and Costs. . Property has three fields i.e. Address, Price, Weekly Rents . Costs has five fileds i.e. Weekly repayments, Strata, Council, Water, Management Fees

CashFlow

Cash flow per week and Cash flow per year are displayed as the output.

Cash flow per year and Cash flow per week is calculated taking  weeklyRent, strata, council, water,managementFees and weeklyRepayments as inputs.

The actual implementation for cash flow per year involves weeklyRent * 52 - (strata + council + water) * 4 - managementFees * 52 - weeklyRepayments * 52.

The actual implementation for cash flow per week involves (weeklyRent * 52 - (strata + council + water) * 4 - managementFees * 52 - weeklyRepayments * 52) / 52
